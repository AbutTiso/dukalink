Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 12, 2026

1. Introduction

DukaLink ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our platform.

2. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us, such as when you:

  • Create an account or register as a vendor
  • Make a purchase or sell products
  • Upload business documents for verification
  • Contact our customer support
  • Subscribe to our newsletters

This information may include your name, email address, phone number, business registration details, KRA PIN, and payment information.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Process your transactions and manage your account
  • Verify your business documents and identity
  • Communicate with you about orders, products, and services
  • Improve our platform and customer experience
  • Comply with legal obligations
  • Prevent fraud and enhance security

4. Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share your information with:

  • Vendors: When you place an order, relevant information is shared with the vendor to fulfill your order
  • Service Providers: Third parties who assist in operating our platform (payment processors, delivery services)
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect our rights

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect your personal information. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access and update your personal information
  • Request deletion of your account
  • Opt-out of marketing communications
  • Download your data

7. Cookies

We use cookies to enhance your browsing experience, analyze site traffic, and personalize content. You can control cookies through your browser settings.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page with an updated effective date.

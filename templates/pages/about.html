Our Mission

To democratize e-commerce for Kenyan small businesses by providing affordable, easy-to-use digital tools that work with existing platforms like WhatsApp and M-Pesa.

No business should be left behind in the digital age.

Our Vision

To become Africa's leading digital commerce enabler, empowering over 1 million small businesses across the continent with accessible e-commerce solutions.

From Kenya to the continent, one shop at a time.

Our Journey

From an idea to empowering thousands of businesses

1
2026

The Beginning

DukaLink was founded in Nairobi, Kenya with a simple idea: help mama mboga and small shops accept orders online without complicated websites.

2
2026

WhatsApp Integration

Launched WhatsApp ordering feature - customers could now browse products and order directly via WhatsApp with auto-generated messages.

3
2026

M-Pesa Integration

Integrated Daraja API for seamless M-Pesa payments. Vendors started receiving instant payment confirmations.

4
2026

1,000 Vendors Milestone

Reached 1,000 active vendors and processed over 50,000 orders. Expanded beyond Nairobi to other counties.

5
2026

The Future

Planning to launch in Uganda and Tanzania, bringing DukaLink's solutions to more African entrepreneurs.
